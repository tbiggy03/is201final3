There are a few keys factors that you will need to consider if you want your training to be as effective as possible. These include:

  • Training
      - Volume - Minimum and Maximum effective volume
      - Exercise Selection
      - Training intensity
  • Dieting
      - Counting calories and macronutrients
      - Your personal caloric maintenance, deficit, and surplus
      - Eating clean, whole foods vs processed/junk foods
  • Recovery
      - Injury prevention
      - Recoverable volume and frequency
      - Joint vs muscle recovery
